# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is designed to guide students, instructors, and lab engineers through a 14-week curriculum covering ROS 2, Gazebo/Unity simulation, NVIDIA Isaac Platform, Vision-Language-Action systems, and culminating in a capstone Autonomous Humanoid Robot project.

## About This Textbook

This textbook follows a structured approach to learning Physical AI and humanoid robotics concepts through:

- **Theoretical Foundations**: Understanding the core concepts and principles
- **Practical Applications**: Hands-on labs and exercises
- **Real-world Examples**: Integration with industry-standard tools and platforms
- **Academic Rigor**: Properly cited sources and peer-reviewed content

## Course Structure

The textbook is organized into several modules:

- **Module 1**: ROS 2 Foundations
- **Module 2**: Gazebo/Unity Simulation
- **Module 3**: NVIDIA Isaac Platform
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action Systems
- **Capstone Project**: Autonomous Humanoid Robot

Each module contains weekly content with associated labs and exercises to reinforce learning.

## Getting Started

To get the most out of this textbook:

1. Follow the modules in sequence to build foundational knowledge
2. Complete all hands-on labs to gain practical experience
3. Review the references and citations for deeper understanding
4. Engage with the capstone project to demonstrate comprehensive knowledge

## Academic Standards

This textbook maintains high academic standards through:

- Proper APA 7 citations for all sources
- Peer-reviewed content and examples
- Evidence-based technical explanations
- Clear learning objectives and assessments

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:

- Undergraduate and graduate students studying AI, robotics, or mechatronics
- Instructors teaching Physical AI or humanoid systems
- Lab engineers setting up ROS 2, Gazebo, Unity, or NVIDIA Isaac environments
