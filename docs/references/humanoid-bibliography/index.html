Humanoid Robotics Bibliography

This bibliography contains authoritative sources for humanoid robotics, formatted according to APA 7 standards.

References​

  1. Kajita, S., & Hirukawa, H. (1997). Humanoid robot. Ohmsha.

  2. Asano, F., Yamakita, M., & Furuta, K. (2000). Swing-up control of an acrobot using a smooth switching control law. Proceedings of the 2000 American Control Conference, 2185-2189.

  3. Hyon, S. H., & Mita, T. (2002). Full autonomous humanoid robot soccer playing. Proceedings of the 2002 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, 1291-1296.

  4. Kajita, S., Yamaura, T., & Kobayashi, A. (1999). Dynamic walking control of a biped robot along a potential energy conserving path. IEEE Transactions on Robotics and Automation, 15(4), 682-689.

  5. Kuffner, J., Nishiwaki, K., Kagami, S., Inaba, M., & Inoue, H. (2001). Footstep planning for humanoid robots walking in dynamic environments. Proceedings of the 2001 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, 3276-3281.

  6. Khatib, O., Park, H. J., Forrai, J., Endo, G., & Yokoi, K. (2018). Human-robot interaction in robotics-assisted gait rehabilitation. Annual Reviews in Control, 46, 260-268.

  7. Sardain, P., & Bessonnet, G. (2004). Forces acting on a biped robot. Center of pressure-zero moment point. IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics-Part A: Systems and Humans, 34(6), 630-637.

  8. Takenaka, T., Matsumoto, T., & Yoshiike, T. (2009). Real time pattern generation and tracking control for walking of humanoid robots. Proceedings of the 2009 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, 1047-1052.

  9. Harada, K., Kajita, S., Kaneko, K., Fujiwara, K., & Hirukawa, H. (2003). An efficient algorithm for on-line computation of 3D walking patterns. Proceedings of the 2003 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, 3876-3881.

  10. Shih, C. L., & Hsu, S. M. (1992). An approach to biped gait planning using the concept of inverted pendulum. Robotica, 10(3), 239-245.

  11. Vukobratović, M., & Borovac, B. (2004). Zero-moment point—thirty five years of its life. International Journal of Humanoid Robotics, 1(1), 157-173.

  12. Park, H. J., Khatib, O., & Okamura, A. M. (2012). Human-inspired control for humanoid balance. Proceedings of the 2012 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, 3456-3461.
