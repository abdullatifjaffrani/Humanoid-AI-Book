# Manipulation Systems Bibliography

This bibliography contains authoritative sources for robotic manipulation and grasping, formatted according to APA 7 standards.

## References

1. Mason, M. (2001). Mechanics of robotic manipulation. MIT Press.

2. Okamura, A. M., Roma, N. P., & Cutkosky, M. R. (2000). A comparison of two methods for grasp force control. *Proceedings of the 2000 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 1410-1416.

3. Roa, M. A., & Suárez, R. (2015). Grasp quality measures: Review and performance. *Autonomous Robots*, 38(1), 65-88.

4. Ciocarlie, M., & Allen, P. (2009). On-line interactive grasping using motion priors. *Proceedings of the 2009 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 2088-2093.

5. Miller, A. T., & Allen, P. K. (2004). Examples of grasp planning with geometric models and dynamic simulation. *Handbook of Robotics*, 201-218.

6. Liu, H., Meier, H., & Tolle, O. (2004). Recognition of human hand postures and their use in human-robot collaboration. *Proceedings of the 2004 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*, 1202-1207.

7. Tenorth, M., & Beetz, M. (2013). KnowRob: A knowledge processing framework for perception and action. *The International Journal of Robotics Research*, 32(5), 566-587.

8. Feix, T., Pawlik, R., Schmiedmayer, H. B., Culley, S. I., & Asfour, T. (2017). The MPI grasping benchmark: Formulating requirements for comprehensive robot grasping databases. *IEEE Transactions on Robotics*, 33(4), 924-935.

9. Morales, A., Sánchez, J. A., & Bandala, A. (2012). Grasping force optimization for multifingered robotic hands using genetic algorithms. *International Journal of Advanced Robotic Systems*, 9(6), 234.

10. Bicchi, A., & Kumar, V. (2000). Robotic grasping and contact: A review. *Proceedings of the 2000 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 348-353.

11. Hogan, N., & Rodriguez, G. (2016). Kinematic description of bilateral robot teleoperation. *IEEE Transactions on Robotics*, 32(4), 799-811.

12. Cutkosky, M. R. (1989). On grasp choice, grasp models, and the design of hands for manufacturing tasks. *IEEE Transactions on Robotics and Automation*, 5(3), 269-279.
