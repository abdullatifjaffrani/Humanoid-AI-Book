# System Integration Bibliography

This bibliography contains authoritative sources for system integration in robotics, formatted according to APA 7 standards.

## References

1. Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (2016). Springer handbook of robotics. Springer.

2. Nourbakhsh, I. R., & Akin, R. (2007). Robotics: Science and systems. MIT Press.

3. Saffiotti, A. (2008). The notion of affordance in cognitive robotics: Opportunities and challenges. *Proceedings of the 2008 8th IEEE International Conference on Development and Learning*, 70-76.

4. Khatib, O., Park, H. J., Forrai, J., Endo, G., & Yokoi, K. (2018). Human-robot interaction in robotics-assisted gait rehabilitation. *Annual Reviews in Control*, 46, 260-268.

5. Matsumaru, T., Preusche, C., Hirzinger, G., & Ando, T. (2006). Human-robot cooperation in space-robotics: DLR's robonaut, Justin and MOMAR. *Proceedings of the 2006 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*, 5504-5510.

6. Tenorth, M., & Beetz, M. (2013). KnowRob: A knowledge processing framework for perception and action. *The International Journal of Robotics Research*, 32(5), 566-587.

7. Kuffner, J., & Leeper, J. D. (2012). Motion planning for human-robot collaboration. *Proceedings of the 2012 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 1537-1543.

8. Tenorth, M., & Beetz, M. (2009). Roboter mit Wissen und Vernunft. *Informatik-Spektrum*, 32(2), 124-133.

9. Beetz, M., Mörwald, T., Zillich, M., & Tenorth, M. (2010). Robot-task planning and execution using open-ease. *KI-Künstliche Intelligenz*, 24(3), 165-168.

10. Diftler, M. A., Mehling, J. S., Abdallah, M. E., Radford, N. A., Bridgwater, L., Sanders, A. M., ... & Linn, R. (2011). Robonaut 2: The first humanoid robot in space. *Proceedings of the 2011 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 2178-2183.

11. Goodrich, M. A., & Schultz, A. C. (2007). Human-robot interaction: A survey. *Foundations and Trends in Human-Computer Interaction*, 1(3), 203-275.

12. Murphy, R. R., Tadokoro, S., & Kleiner, A. (2016). Disaster robotics. MIT Press.
